Character Database

These are my characters that I plan on using in some sort of future comic... It's been something I've been randomly building on since my freshman year of highschool. It's just gonna be a slasher BL or something in a cyber/dystopic setting... Nothing too serious though cuz I can't write that well. I don't really like writing descriptions because I feel embarrassed, preferring to show their traits through drawings and doodles, but I do want to write a little bit about what I was thinking when I made them.

I have a tendency to design my characters in pairs and occasionally in a group of 3 sometimes. A proper gallery page for each character will be made at some point!

Please click the portrait to enter a character's gallery~☆☆

NAME: SID

AGE: 25

HEIGHT: 193 cm

DOB: 04/07

A contract killer who is aloof externally, but is incredibly gap moe. :3

Design Notes
  • He has two outfits: his initial "skin" mask and orange jumpsuit, and his second more "tactical" design.
  • He's a few years younger in his skin mask design but I can't ever decide on his age. His second design is actually supposed to be somewhere in the 30s. For now, his younger design is 24~25.
  • Evidently, the first design is super Slipknot inspired. The second one is a combination of Dorohedoro and Blame!
  • His load-bearing vest in his tactical design is based on the Individual Integrated Fighting System (IIFS).
  • He's one of my favorite and most personal OCs, who reflects myself the most. A lot of my drawings of him help me process my feelings of dysmorphia and struggles with self-image.


NAME: NEENZ

AGE: 2X

HEIGHT: 175 cm

DOB: 01/16

A contract killer working with Sid. Easygoing but behaves erratically.

Design Notes
  • His design inspiration is Keith Flint from The Prodigy.
  • During his first design drafts, he had a stereotypically British punk appearance similar to Nobuo Terashima from Nana. Although he kept the spiked hair, I changed the British punk aspect because I think that there are enough characters that fit that archetype and appearance.
  • In the beginning, he wasn't supposed to be a contract killer. He was just a boutique employee...
  • Initially, he was supposed to wear a shirt with a different slogan every time (like Travis from Killer7).

NAME: KAZ

AGE: 28

HEIGHT: 178cm

DOB: 08/06

Works with Mal at his shop, who also made his prosthetic arm. Characterized by an unusually strong sense of loyalty.

Design Notes
  • He's named after Kazuma Kaneko.
  • I don't draw him as much anymore, but he's important to me because he began my artstyle shift into what it is today. Before that, I drew in a 70s-80s shoujou/josei style inspired by Keiko Takemiya, Riyoko Ikeda, Hagio Moto, etc.
  • He also has two designs like Sid (to be uploaded), with the second giving him a new prosthetic and a shaved head. The updated prosthetic design is inspired by horseshoe crabs.

NAME: HÅKAN

AGE: Late 30s

HEIGHT: 185cm

DOB: 06/26

Founder and leader of a PMC called Segervittring. His eyes hold a sinister quality to them despite his cordial and charming persona. Has strange tastes.

Design Notes
  • His first designs were inspired by Raymond Watts from PIG/KMFDM/Schwein.
  • His younger design is based on the field uniform of the National People's Army.
  • From the beginning, he always had a hat based on the Prussian Landwehrmütze cap and a day collar inspired by the brand Swedish Collar.
  • He was designed with bondage/leather culture and a military silhouette in mind, but also evolved to be a reflection of my love of uniforms from the German Democratic Republic.
  • I used to mentally flagellate myself over sexuality, so he's also an important OC to me because he is a means for me to kind of take control and liberate myself of the guilt I used to feel about it.

NAME: JAN

AGE: Late 30s

HEIGHT: 183cm

DOB: 02/07

Member of Håkan's PMC. High-strung, meticulous, and a little bit pathetic.

NAME: KATYUSHA
