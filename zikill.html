Zi:Kill Scans

The first 3 Zi:Kill albums might be the best albums ever to me... I love their 1989-1991 post-punkesque sound that discontinued when they released Rocket and In The Hole... I know those are one of their most popular albums but to be honest, I really don't like them... However, they're still a really solid band to me and Ken, Seiichi, and Tusk are really talented at what they do. All of their drummers are great too but personally the Yukihiro era is my favorite. And Tusk is still one of my great loves... Anyways, here are some scans of their fanclub stuff, flyers, clippings, etc. that I have. I have a pretty hefty amount but most of them are too big for me to scan so unfortunately I'm not able to get to a lot of them right now, but hopefully I can in the future. There's also some random stuff like New Years cards and Birthday cards that maybe I'll get to later.
