Hello, and welcome to my website! Here's a little bit of info about me:

I'm a Chinese-American university student double majoring in German and European Studies orz. My hobbies are drawing, playing games, itabags, listening to music, and amateur archiving. I also love J-fashion and have been wearing J-punk since 2019 and Ouji/EGA since 2022.

I don't really know HTML or CSS but I figured it would be a fun hobby to pick up. I've been messing around trying to make sites since 2017, but it wasn't until 2022 that I really stuck with this one! Since I don't have active social media accounts, I made this site with the intention of replacing the functions that it used to serve, such as hosting my art, maintaining a blog, and sharing things like my artbook/doujinshi scans. Ultimately, I hope that it's someplace that is fun and interesting to browse around. Feel free to send an email if you would like to correspond about anything.
