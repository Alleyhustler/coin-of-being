Artwork Archive

Here's where I'll share artwork I created when I first started drawing. For a little background, I'm self-taught and the very first manga that inspired me to draw was Yotsubato. I've gone through a lot of style changes, and I always find looking back on my progression quite funny.

Touhou

Touhou art I made from around 2010-2015. I was still a child when I drew these, so they are scattered with some silly sidenotes. One of my friends recalled that I used to draw Reimu everyday for years in elementary school... Even though I don't talk about the series that much anymore, it is still something I love a lot.

Digital

I used to draw with a mouse using a website called RateMyDrawings (RIP). It wasn't until around 2014 I started drawing on Paint Tool Sai with a refurbished tablet.
