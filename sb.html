Electric Body Press (Fanclub)

Soft Ballet's fanclub, Electric Body Press. I only have volumes 1-19 (missing 20-22). They gradually go from 1 pages, to double sided, to 8 pages, to 12 pages. By the way, on volumes such as 8 and 9, it was held together by glue instead of staples. If you decided to buy it second-hand, just be aware there's a highly likely chance the pages will be loose.


  • No. 11, 12, 13, and 16 have certain pages omitted because they contain sensitive information pertaining to the members.
  • Pages with pictures of fans have been removed or edited out to protect their identity.
  • Fan names have been removed.

I can't read Japanese so I may have missed some names. If you see anything that might be sensitive towards Soft Ballet's fans or members, please let me know so I can remove it. Thanks!
